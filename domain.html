The choice of the topic

The idea and the reasons

The massacre of Bologna is one of the most tragic events in the so called italian "Anni di piombo" (Years of Lead). In the period included between the '60s and the '80s the the world wide opposition between communism and fascism reversed in the yet difficult italian political situation all its worst consequencies. The cultural objects describing the event
