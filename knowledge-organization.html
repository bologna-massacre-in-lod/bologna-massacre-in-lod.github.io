Knowledge Organization

From KOP (Knowledge Organization Process) to KOS (Knowledge Organization System).

ER MODEL

The Entity- Relationship describes interrelated things of interest in a specific domain of knowledge. A basic ER model is composed of entity types (which classify the things of interest) and specifies relationships that can exist between entities. We drew two different models as representation of the desire to highlight the evolution of understading that we have had.
