METADATA ANALYSIS

Our metadata analysis was carried out as a more in-depth study of our selected items, considering both the person, family or corporate body that physically or legally owns them and the provider through which we have found them. For what concerns the standards, we decided to proceed in two ways: if the cultural institution provider of the item declares the metadata standard, we kept it; while we arbitrary chose the most suitable ones, among all the standards generally used in the cultural field, if we were not able to find the standards used to describe and catalogue the item itself (or to avoid repetitions).
